Hi! I'm Nathan, a sophomore majoring in Artificial Intelligence at the Carnegie Mellon University School of Computer Science. I've always been excited about technology, and I fell in love with CS upon realizing it's limitless potential for creativity and it's ability to bring people together.

I interned at Fanhouse where I built a weighted social graph experienced by over 65,000 users and trained a neural network for the content moderation of over 30,000 monthly photo uploads.

Some projects I've worked on include a 3D ping pong game, an ML classifier for illegal stock market orders , and a study app that leverages computer vision. Currently, I'm working with Christopher Porco to build WriteAway, a simple brainstorming and writing environment.

I was a TA for 15112: Fundamentals of CS, where I lead a recitation of 25 students and ran a hackathon involving 200+ students. I also document my life and give college application advice on my Youtube channel.
